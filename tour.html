Schedule a Tour

Come walk our halls.

The best way to know if Franklin Court is the right fit is to visit — meet our team, see an apartment, ask every question that matters. Tours take about an hour and are scheduled by appointment.

We'd love to have you stay for lunch in the dining room. Most families do.

What to expect

Four steps, no pressure.

STEP 1

Reach out

A 10-minute call with our admissions director to learn about availability and answer your initial questions.

STEP 2

Visit

About an hour at our Bristol campus. See two or three apartments, walk the courtyard, meet our team.

STEP 3

Stay for lunch

Our chef prepares lunch every day. You'll eat with residents and staff — the best way to feel our community.

STEP 4

Decide on your terms

No pressure. We'll follow up once, and only if you ask us to. This is your decision, on your timeline.

Request a tour

Pick a day that works for you.

Fill out the form below and our admissions director will reach out within one business day to confirm your visit.

Prefer to talk it through first?

Most families find it useful to chat with our admissions director before scheduling a visit. No commitment, no follow-up unless you ask for one.
