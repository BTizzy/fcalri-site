Accessibility

Our commitment to accessibility.

Franklin Court Assisted Living is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Standards we follow

This website aims to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities, and user-friendly for everyone.

Specific measures

  • Body text is set at 18px or larger (1.125rem) for comfortable reading
  • Color contrast ratios meet WCAG AA across text and interactive elements
  • Skip-link at the top of every page for screen reader and keyboard users
  • Semantic HTML5 (header, nav, main, footer) used consistently
  • ARIA labels on icon-only buttons and decorative SVGs hidden
  • All forms have explicit, visible labels
  • Phone numbers and hours are above-the-fold on every page
  • Focus styles are visible on all interactive elements
  • Site is fully usable with keyboard alone

Ongoing work

This is an actively maintained site. We test with automated accessibility tools and review changes against our accessibility checklist before deployment. We welcome your feedback on how we can improve accessibility for everyone.

Facility accessibility

The physical residence at 180 Franklin Street is fully ADA compliant — single-story layout, all-doorways wheelchair accessible, grab bars in every bathroom, ramp access throughout, and visual and auditory alert systems in resident apartments. Call our admissions director to discuss specific accessibility needs.
