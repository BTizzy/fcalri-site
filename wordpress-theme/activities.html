Six programming pillars

Designed with intention, not just occupation.

Our activities program is built around six pillars. Each week we aim to offer all six — so residents have something physically engaging, mentally stimulating, creatively fulfilling, socially connected, spiritually meaningful, and community-connected.

Move

Physical

Daily exercise class, walking group in the courtyard, stretches, balance, and chair yoga.

Make

Creative

Painting, ceramics, flower arranging, cooking club — making things for the joy of making.

Connect

Social

Trivia, game nights, ice cream socials, themed dinners, and one-on-one time with our activities team.

Think

Intellectual

Newspaper roundtables, documentaries, guest speakers, literary discussion, and weekly brain-games.

Reflect

Spiritual

Optional worship services, devotional time, music ministry, and quiet contemplation.

Belong

Community

Outings to Linden Place, Bristol Bagel, the East Bay Bike Path, and seasonal events like the 4th of July Parade.

Take a look at our activity board.

If you're local, drop in any Tuesday or Thursday afternoon at 2 PM for bingo or trivia and stay for coffee. You can meet our activities director and the residents who show up most.
