Careers

Work where the care is real.

Franklin Court is small — about 30 residents — and intentionally so. That scale shapes everything about how our team operates: short hallways, real relationships with the people in our care, and the kind of day-to-day autonomy you can't find at a 200-bed facility.

We're hiring across clinical care, dining, and housekeeping. Competitive wages, benefits for full-time, and a workplace that treats its employees like family.

Why Franklin Court

What's different about working here.

Small by design

Real relationships

You'll know every resident by name, by preference, by family. No hurrying from room to room at a 200-bed campus.

Stability

Long-tenured staff

Many of our employees have been here 10+ years. That kind of continuity is rare in the industry and worth something.

Mission-driven

Nonprofit parent

Operated by East Bay Community Development Corp, a Bristol-based 501(c)(3) — surplus revenue reinvested in the mission.

Predictable hours

Real schedules

Published schedules 2+ weeks out. We respect that you have a life outside of work — overtime is the exception, not the norm.

Benefits

Health, dental, retirement

Comprehensive benefits for full-time employees plus paid time off and tuition support for continuing education.

Team-first

Supportive leadership

Angela (Administrator), Amy (Nursing Director), and Michael (Admissions) keep an open door and respond quickly when you need help.

Open positions

We're currently hiring.

All positions are at our Bristol campus. Full-time, part-time, and per-diem shifts available for some roles.

Clinical

Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) — Full-Time

Provide high-quality nursing care to residents under the direction of the Wellness Director. Med-pass, assessments, family communications.

  • Active Rhode Island LPN license required
  • Two shifts available: 7 AM – 3 PM and 3 PM – 11 PM
  • Every other weekend rotation
Email Amy · Wellness Director
Clinical

Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) — All Shifts

Help residents with activities of daily living — bathing, dressing, grooming, mobility, meals. Direct care at the heart of our community.

  • Active Rhode Island CNA certification required
  • Day, evening, and overnight shifts available
  • Sign-on bonus available for full-time hires
Email Amy · Wellness Director
Dining

Dietary Aide / Server — Part-Time

Three meals a day, served with warmth. Help our Unidine dining team plate, serve, and clean up.

  • No certification required; will train
  • AM, midday, and dinner shifts
  • Good fit for parents, students, semi-retired
Email Jacqui · Dining Director
Housekeeping

Housekeeper — Full-Time

Keep our residents' apartments and common spaces clean, comfortable, and welcoming. Weekly apartment cleaning plus public-area rotation.

  • No prior experience required; will train
  • Monday – Friday, 8 AM – 4 PM
  • Health & dental benefits available
Email Ana · Housekeeping Director
Activities

Activities Assistant — Per-Diem

Help our Activities Director plan and run programs. Lead small group activities, accompany residents on outings, drive the activity van.

  • Experience in recreation or activities preferred
  • Flexible scheduling, weekday and weekend shifts
  • Clean driving record required for outings
Email Debra · Activities Director
"I've been here for sixteen years. What keeps me here isn't the work — it's the people. Our residents become like family, and so does the team. When I come to work, I know who's having a hard day and who's having a good one, and I can respond to that. You don't get that at the bigger places."
Pat S. · Activities Coordinator · 16 years at Franklin Court
