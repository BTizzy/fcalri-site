Apartments

Your private space, beautifully yours.

Franklin Court offers a variety of attractive apartments, each featuring a full bath and kitchenette. All apartments are designed to provide comfortable living space and privacy — and residents add a personal touch with their own furniture and treasured belongings.

Included in every apartment

Designed for comfort and safety.

Each apartment comes with thoughtful features that make daily living easier and your apartment feel like home:

  • Kitchenette with refrigerator-freezer, two-burner cook top, microwave, and sink
  • Large bathroom featuring walk-in shower with built-in seat and safety grab bars
  • Wall-to-wall carpeting
  • Expansive windows that provide natural light — blinds and valances included
  • Cathedral ceilings with attractive lighting and ceiling fan fixture
  • Wired for cable television and telephone service
  • 24-hour personal emergency call response
  • Individually controlled heating system
  • State-of-the-art fire/smoke detectors and sprinkler system throughout
A note on personalization

Furnish with what you love.

Your apartment is your home, not a hotel room. We encourage residents to bring their own furniture, photos, art, and treasured belongings. Cathedral ceilings comfortably accommodate larger pieces. We'll help you arrange the room safely during move-in.

Living

Bring what makes you comfortable

Armchairs, side tables, your favorite reading lamp. Family heirlooms that tell your story.

Decor

Make it yours visually

Photos, paintings, throws, rugs — anything you'd have in a place you call home.

Safety

We help with the rest

Our team will walk through safety considerations — cords, rugs, furniture placement — with you and your family.

Ready to see them in person?

Tours take about an hour. You'll see furnished model apartments, talk with our team about available units, and leave with a clear next step.
