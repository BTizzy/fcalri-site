Privacy Policy

Effective: July 1, 2026 · Last updated: July 8, 2026

What we collect

This Privacy Policy describes how Franklin Court Assisted Living (operated by East Bay Community Development Corp) collects, uses, and protects information you provide when using this website or contacting our team.

Information you provide directly

When you fill out a contact or tour-scheduling form, we collect the information you've entered — typically your name, email, phone number, and any message body. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry and to follow up about admissions, employment, or other requests you've initiated.

Information collected automatically

This site uses no third-party analytics. The hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may log basic request metadata (IP address, user agent, pages visited) for security and operational purposes. We do not use this data for marketing.

Cookies

This site does not set tracking cookies. Functional cookies may be set by your browser or by the Google Fonts CDN.

HIPAA & resident health information

This website is for general inquiries only. It is not a covered entity for HIPAA purposes and does not collect protected health information. All resident care documentation, medical records, and protected health information is handled through separate, secure clinical systems not connected to this website.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we've collected from you through this website. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
